1. Introduction

Welcome to Jyotiba Phule Family, an initiative by Sankalp Seva Sansthan. This app is designed to support community development, donations, and membership registration. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number when you register.
  • Payment details for donations and MetroMoney registration fees (processed securely through third-party providers).
  • Basic app usage data for improvement of our services.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Enable donations and membership registration.
  • Communicate updates, receipts, and support responses.
  • Improve our app’s features and services.

4. Donations & Payments

Donations and registration fees are processed via trusted third-party payment gateways. We do not store sensitive payment details such as card numbers or UPI pins on our servers.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, please visit: Delete My Account

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
